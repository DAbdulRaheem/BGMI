Welcome to My BGMI India Blog

Hello! I'm Raheem aka (TAxALEX), a huge fan of Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI). This blog is all about the latest updates, tournaments, and my favorite moments in the game.

BGMI is a battle royale game developed and published by Krafton. It is a mobile version of PUBG Mobile, specifically designed for the Indian audience. The game features various modes, including classic battle royale, arcade, and team deathmatch. Players can team up with friends or play solo to compete against others in a shrinking map until only one player or team remains.
